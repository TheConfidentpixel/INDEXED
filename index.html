Mineralogy is the study of minerals and their properties, while petrology is the study of rocks and their origins.
A general geology course is a college-level class that provides an introduction to the field of geology, which is the study of the Earth, its composition, and its history.
An ore deposit and economic minerals course is a college-level class that focuses on the study of minerals that have economic value, such as those that are mined for their use in industry or as sources of raw materials.
Principles of metallurgy is a course that focuses on the science and technology of metals and alloys, including their properties, behavior, and processing.
An introduction to mining engineering course is a college-level class that provides an overview of the field of mining engineering.
Introductory Mining Engineering is a textbook that provides an overview of the field of mining engineering. It covers a range of topics related to the exploration, extraction, and processing of minerals and other natural resources from the earth.
How Mining Works explains complex mining concepts in a way simple enough for those who are not familiar with the industry, yet thorough enough to be useful to long-time professionals.
A mining reference handbook is a book that provides information and guidance on a range of topics related to the exploration, extraction, and processing of minerals and other natural resources from the earth.
INDEXED is a website for Mining Engineers
